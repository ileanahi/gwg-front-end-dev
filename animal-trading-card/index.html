Hawaiian Monk Seal

The Hawaiian Monk Seal is one of Hawaii's two native mammals.

  • Diet: Fish, lobster, octopus, and squid
  • Habits: Night hunter, day sunbather
  • Life expectancy: 25 - 30 years
  • Status: Endangered

The Hawaiian Monk seal is a grey, slender, torpedo shaped seal. They have relatively small heads with large black eyes. On average, the males grow up to 7 feet in length and the females grow up to 8 feet in length.
